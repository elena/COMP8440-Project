       CLUG Programmers' Special Interest Group Meeting - 12th April 2012
       ==================================================================

Date:           12th April 2012 (Second Thursday of the month)

Time:           19:00 - 21:00 (or when it finishes)

Speaker:        James Iseppi

Abstract:
               James will be leading a tutorial in installing and
               hacking on Zookeepr.

               Zookeepr is the system that runs the Linux Conference
               Australia. It does everything from collecting talk and
               tutorial proposals, handling the reviews, constructing a
               schedule, taking registrations and providing information
               at the conference.

               Please make sure you have the pre-requisites installed
               by following the instructions at:

               https://github.com/zookeepr/zookeepr/blob/master/INSTALL




Venue:          Room N101
               Computer Science and Information Technology Building
               North Road
               The Australian National University

               See http://clug.org.au/ for more directions and a map

Food/drink:     Pizza and soft drink/juice. Come hungry, and bring
               about $6 to cover the cost of your share if you
               want some.

If you would like to give a talk at a future meeting, please email me.
